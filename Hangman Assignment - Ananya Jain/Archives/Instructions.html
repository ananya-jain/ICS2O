Instructions

To go along with this Hangman Game, there are a few simple steps...

1. Click on "Start Game" to generate a random word and display the letters to choose from.
2. Enter your name in the alert.
3. Pick a letter at a time to guess the chosen word.
4. Keep picking letters until you either lose all your 7 lives, or guess the word.
5. If you wish to play again, simply click on any of the following: "Level 1", "Level 2", and "Level 3".

Note: If at any time you wish for a hint, click on "Hint". There is only 1 hint per word. Your wins and losses will be recorded on the top right corner. Level 1 has words of 3-5 letters. Level 2 has words of 6 or more letter. Level 3 has the option to guess a component or animal of the sea made up of 2 words.

Click on "Hangman Game" to access the game. Enjoy!
